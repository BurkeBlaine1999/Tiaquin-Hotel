Here in the Tiaquin hotel we strive to provide our customers with the best possible experiences .

Formal ~ 40 euro a night

The formal suite is located between the first and third floors of the hotel.
The suit contains one double bed ,Disabled bathroom with shower and bath , a 42 inch plasma screen television with access to Basic Sky channels. There are also closets to store you're clothes and belongings.
Room Service is available at this suite from 9am to 10pm.


premium ~ 50 euro a night

The premium suite is located between the fourth and fifth floors of the hotel.
The suit contains one King size bed ,Disabled bathroom with shower and bath , a 45 inch plasma screen television with access to Basic Sky channels and Sky Movies. There are also large closets to store you're clothes and belongings. Also Included is a kettle with tea bags and fresh milk.
Room Service is available at this suite from 9am to 10pm .


Deluxe ~ 70 euro a night

The Deluxe suite is located between the sixth and seventh floors of the hotel.
The suit contains one King size bed ,Disabled bathroom with shower , bath and Hairdriers, a 50 inch plasma screen television with access to all of Sky. There are also large closets to store you're clothes and belongings. Also Included is a coffee and tea making machine and a stocked mini fridge!
Room Service is available at this suite from 9am to 12pm .


Luxury ~ 100 euro a night

The Luxury suite is located on the top floor of the hotel.
The suit contains one King size bed ,Disabled bathroom with shower, bath ,Hairdriers and a jacuzzi! a HD 70 inch plasma screen television with access to all of Sky. There are also large closets to store you're clothes and belongings, a coffee and tea making machine ,a stocked mini fridge and your own personal bar with a cocktail specialist on call.
